One-Time Setup
1
Create your Google Sheet
One Sheet, two tabs. Name tab 1 Rounds, tab 2 Journal.
Rounds tab — Row 1 headers:
Date | Course | Tees | Rating | Slope | Weather | Temp | Wind | Time | Score | Putts | FIR% | FIR Left% | FIR Right% | FIR Short% | FIR Long% | FIR Miss Quality | GIR% | GIR Left% | GIR Right% | GIR Short% | GIR Long% | GIR Miss Quality | 1Putt% | 2Putt% | 3Putt% | OB Off Tee | OB Approach | Penalty | Par3 Avg | Par4 Avg | Par5 Avg | Up&Downs
Journal tab — Row 1 headers:
Date | Course | Notes | Best Part | Biggest Miss | Carry Forward | Round ID
2
Share your Sheet publicly (Viewer)
Share → Anyone with the link → Viewer. This lets the app read it without login.
3
Connect your Google Sheet
Paste your Sheet ID — the string between /d/ and /edit in the URL.
4
Add your Anthropic API key
Powers scorecard photo parsing AND the pre-round brief. Get a free key at console.anthropic.com. Stored locally in your browser only.
5
Create Google Forms for mobile input (optional but recommended)
Create two Google Forms — one for each tab — and link them to your Sheet via Responses → Link to Sheets. This lets you log from your phone without opening this app.
📱 Phone workflow: Fill Google Form after your round → open this app → Dashboard → Sync to see your updated data.

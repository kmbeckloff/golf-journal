One-time setup
1
Create your Google Sheet
Row 1 must have these exact column headers:
Date  |  Course  |  Score  |  Conditions  |  Notes  |  Best part  |  Biggest miss  |  Carry forward
2
Create your Google Form (for mobile logging)
Add 8 fields matching the columns above. In the Form go to Responses → Link to Sheets and connect it to your Sheet. This is your phone input.
3
Make the Sheet publicly readable
Share → Anyone with the link → Viewer. This lets the app read it without a login.
4
Connect your Sheet
Paste your Sheet ID — the string between /d/ and /edit in your Sheet URL.
5
Add your Anthropic API key (for AI brief)
Get a free key at console.anthropic.com. Stored locally in your browser — never sent anywhere except Anthropic's API.
